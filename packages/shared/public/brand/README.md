Brand gold hex `#BEAF88` (Relentless Gold, Pantone 2324 C) fails AA contrast on cream `#FAF7F2` (2.07:1). Per the plan's Brand truth section, the gold wordmark is only used on ink or deep-olive backgrounds; on light backgrounds the wordmark renders black (or the version's equivalent dark ink).
